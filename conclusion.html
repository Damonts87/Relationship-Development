Conclusion

Looking at several of my relationships with the Social Penetration Theory has helped me better understand the role of communication in relationships. It helped me realize how important trust is in growing a relationship. Each of my relationships I looked at grew after gaining a new level of trust. I will now be able to utilize these theories better in other and future relationships I have with people.
